6. Define the first-order Taylor approximation at the selected point.¶

  • Taylor approximation .

  • first-order Taylor approximation of ${f}$ , near point ${z}$:

    • ${\hat f(x) = f(z)+\frac{\partial f}{\partial x_1}(x_1 - z_1) + \dotsb + \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_n}(x_n - z_n)}$

    • ${\hat f(x)}$ is very close to ${f(x)}$ when ${x_i}$ are all near ${z_i}$

    • ${\hat f(x)}$ is an affine function of ${x}$

  • inner product .

    • ${\hat f(x) = f(z) + \nabla f(z)^T(x -z)}$

    • ${\nabla f(z) = \big( \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_1}(z) + \dotsb + \frac{\partial f}{\partial x_n}(z) \big)}$
